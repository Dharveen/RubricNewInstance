# Rubric

## Project Overview
Rubric is a comprehensive assessment platform designed to bring clarity, consistency, and efficiency to the evaluation process. By leveraging standardized digital rubrics, the platform helps educators and managers provide high-quality feedback and track performance trends over time.

## Mission Statement
Our mission is to empower organizations to foster growth and excellence through transparent and objective assessment frameworks. We believe that clear expectations and consistent evaluation are the cornerstones of sustainable digital and educational ecosystems.
